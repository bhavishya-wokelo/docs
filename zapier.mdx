## Overview

Wokelo's Zapier integration connects AI-powered research to your existing tools — CRMs, spreadsheets, project management systems, and more. Automate research workflows and eliminate manual data entry across 8,000\+ apps.

## Setting Up a Zap (Universal Steps)

All Wokelo Zaps follow the same four-step pattern:

**Step 1 — Create a Trigger** Log into [zapier.com](http://zapier.com), click **Zap**, then **Trigger**. Choose the app and event that starts your workflow (e.g., "New row in Google Sheets", "New lead in HubSpot").

**Step 2 — Add Wokelo as an Action** Click **Action**, search for **Wokelo AI**, and select it. If it's your first time, authenticate and connect your Wokelo account.

**Step 3 — Configure the Action** Select the specific Wokelo action, then map the required inputs from your trigger data. Press `/` to browse available dynamic fields from your connected tools.

**Step 4 — Use the Output** Every action returns a **Report ID** and **Report URL**. Map these to your tools — save to a CRM field, update a spreadsheet column, or chain into another action (e.g., download the report, then upload to Google Drive).

## Best Practices

- **Validate inputs:** Ensure website URLs include `https://` and industry names are specific (e.g., "B2B SaaS" not just "Software").
- **Use the Report Completion trigger** before downloading — only attempt downloads on completed reports.
- **Chain actions** for end-to-end automation: create → monitor completion → download → distribute.
- **Store Report IDs** in your CRM or spreadsheet for future reference and audit tracking.
- **For file uploads:** maximum 10 files per request, 20MB per file. Use the returned success string as input for custom workflow actions.
