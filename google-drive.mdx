## Overview

Connect your Google Drive to Wokelo and seamlessly convert your existing documents into AI-powered research insights. Through a secure authentication connection, select files directly from Google Drive and feed them into our research agents to generate actionable analysis—no data migration required.

## Integration Capabilities

- **Comprehensive file support** — Word, PowerPoint, Excel, PDF, text, CSV.
- **Preserves your workflow** — Works with existing folder structures without reorganization
- **Enterprise security** — Fully encrypted and SOC 2 Type II compliant

## Use-cases

- **Due diligence research** — Analyze CIMs, board decks, and KPI files for comprehensive company assessments
- **Custom workflow integration** — Incorporate Google Drive documents into tailored research processes
- **Intelligent document querying** — Ask natural language questions like "What is the latest ARR by region?" across your file library
- **Report generation** — Create investor briefs, DDQs, and presentation decks directly from selected Google Drive content
