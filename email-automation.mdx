Wokelo's email integration allows you to create Company and Industry Research reports just by sending an email.

## How it works

### Company Research

1. Send us an email at [research@wokelo.ai](mailto:research@wokelo.ai) with the following details:\
   **Subject:** Company Research \
   **Email Content:** Company Name, Company Website
   <Frame>
     ![Image](/images/image-28.png)
   </Frame>
2. Your Company Research report will be initiated for the matched company
   <Frame>
     ![Image](/images/image-35.png)
   </Frame>
   1. If we find multiple companies that match the company name, we’ll request you to select the intended company.
   <Frame>
     ![Image](/images/image-36.png)
   </Frame>
   2. Once you click on the intended company, an email will be auto-generated with the request's details. You can send that email as is and your report will be initiated.
   <Frame>
     ![Image](/images/image-38.png)
   </Frame>
   ### Industry Research
   1. Send us an email at [research@wokelo.ai](mailto:research@wokelo.ai) with the following details: \
      **Subject:** Industry Research \
      **Email Content:** Industry Name
   <Frame>
     ![Image](/images/image-31.png)
   </Frame>
   2. Wokelo will confirm the initiation of the report and share the report pdf once generated.
   <Frame>
     ![Image](/images/image-34.png)
   </Frame>
