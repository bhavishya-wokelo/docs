## Overview

Wokelo seamlessly integrates with DealCloud to auto-generate research and enrich your deal pipeline in a secure, compliant way. By bridging due diligence with relationship and deal management, we eliminate manual data entry and embed updated, context-aware insights directly into your Entries—empowering your team to evaluate opportunities faster than before.

## Key Capabilities & Workflows

### **Enterprise-Grade Security**

We operate on a restricted access model, connecting only to the specific Pipelines, EntryTypes, and Fields you explicitly authorize. Your sensitive deal data, private notes, and relationship intelligence remain completely isolated from the integration.

### Intelligent Pipeline Triggers

Wokelo connects to DealCloud's event system, enabling automated research at critical deal milestones:

- **Entry Creation** — Generate instant company profiles when new Companies or Deals are added
- **Pipeline Stage Changes** — Trigger due diligence reports when deals advance to DD or IC stages
- **Bespoke Workflow Execution** — We execute your custom research workflows and write structured outputs back into DealCloud.

### Seamless Insight Enrichment

Wokelo writes insights directly back into DealCloud. We push findings from your custom workflows to Entry fields, deal records, or Activity logs—keeping your data decision-ready at all times.

## Use Cases

- **New Entry Added** — When a new company or target is created in DealCloud, Wokelo automatically initiates the appropriate research workflow. There is no need for manual requests; the background analysis starts the moment the record appears.
- **Status Change** — As a deal moves through your pipeline stages, Wokelo detects the status change and triggers the specific depth of analysis required for that new phase.
- **Insights Enrichment** — Wokelo pushes key findings directly back into your DealCloud fields and grids.
